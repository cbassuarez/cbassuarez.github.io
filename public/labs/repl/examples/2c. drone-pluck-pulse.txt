// drone + pluck + pulse voices, staggered into a small arrangement.
// Drone is second-order: one block keeps sounding and retunes on committed leaves.
// Pluck and pulse are first-order: each committed leaf makes a clear event.
// `enter`/`exit` windows arrange them: the drone opens alone, the pluck
// arrives, then the pulse comes in late and bows out before the rest.

tempo 84
meter 4/4
tuning kirnberger-3
eval   reset

drone   A2   ~    C3   ~
speed   1
gain    0.24
decay   5.5
tone    dark
harm    pair
pan     center
space   0.28
chorus  drift
// no enter/exit — the drone is the bedrock for the whole piece.

// scoped handoff: this and following pitched blocks use the new tuning.
tuning 12-tet-edo 432

pluck   *4   (*4 A*)   .   ~
speed   1
gain    0.42
decay   0.58
tone    bright
harm    pair
pan     left center right center
enter   4 bars

pulse   *    .    *    .
speed   1
gain    0.34
decay   0.055
tone    bright
crush   8
resolution 0.35
pan     right center left center
enter   8 bars
exit    20 bars
