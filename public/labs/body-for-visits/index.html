the work

A single shared body. A visible visit, after three seconds of foreground dwell, appends one grammar-aware token. There is no form. There is no input. The medium is the visited networked surface.

privacy

Only a hashed session token and a hashed address are retained — enough to prevent accidental repeated contribution and nothing more. No raw addresses. No full user-agents. No fingerprinting. No analytics.

preservation

The live work is what is in front of you. The event journal is the work's append-only substrate. The static snapshot is documentation, not the work itself — useful for citation, not for experiencing the piece.
