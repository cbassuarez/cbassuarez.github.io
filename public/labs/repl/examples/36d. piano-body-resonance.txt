// piano body resonance — the piano as a room, not a single sample.
//
// piano-only surfaces shape the *body* the samples sit inside:
//   - sympathetic: cross-string vibration. high values let any struck
//                  string excite the rest of the harp underneath.
//   - lid:         how openly the box radiates highs. wide = bright.
//   - una:         the soft pedal. dampens upper partials, softens timbre.
//   - stretch:     octave stretch. the slight detune real pianos have at
//                  the extremes — bass slightly flat, treble slightly sharp.
//   - release:     release tail length. long values let the body keep
//                  ringing after the dampers should have caught it.
//
// three blocks: a low pedal point, a high pad, and a single ghost note
// way up in the treble. they all share the same body model (lid/sympathetic
// /stretch) so they ring each other through the simulated harp.

tempo 56
meter 4/4

// 1. low pedal point — anchored, slow, deeply pedaled, ringing low.
piano   C2   .    .    .    |   .    .    F2   .    |   A2   .    .    .    |   .    .    E2   .
force   pp
layer   xfade
pedal   0.92
sympathetic 0.85
lid     0.95
una     0.42
release 0.85
stretch 0.55
human   0.10

// 2. quiet treble pad — vibrates sympathetically with the bass below.
piano   .    .    E5   G5   |   C6   .    .    .    |   .    G5   E5   .    |   C5   .    .    G5
force   pp
layer   xfade
pedal   0.92
sympathetic 0.95
lid     0.95
una     0.65
release 0.90
stretch 0.55
human   0.14
pan     0.3

// 3. ghost note — a single very-high tone every other bar, exposing the
//    sympathetic resonance of the harp underneath.
piano   .    .    .    .    |   E6   .    .    .    |   .    .    .    .    |   .    .    G6   .
force   pp
layer   xfade
pedal   1
sympathetic 1
lid     1
una     0.7
release 1
stretch 0.55
human   0.18
pan     -0.35
enter   4 bars

// TWEAK IDEAS
//   - drop `sympathetic` to 0 on every block — the body collapses to
//     three isolated samples, no shared ring.
//   - close the lid: `lid 0.15` everywhere darkens the room.
//   - turn `stretch` off (`stretch 0`) — the extremes stop sounding
//     "real" and start sounding mathematically perfect.
//   - lift `force` to `mf` on block 1 — the bass becomes the loud
//     event and you hear the treble blocks sympathize with it.
